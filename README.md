# Tally Ballatro
![Tally Ballatro mod cover image](https://i.imgur.com/59nyOqa.png)\
A Balatro mod about everyone's favorite band, Tally Hall! This is a moderately sized content mod that adds new jokers, tarots, spectrals, and enhancements which reference different aspects of everyone's favorite indie band, Tally Hall!

## Newest version: 1.4.0!

The latest version of Tally Ballatro introduces one new enhancement, and a new tarot and spectral card!

### Full changelog for 1.4.0:

New content:
 - One new enhancement: Blossom. Each Blossom card gives x1 chips. Scales by x0.05 for each Blossom card in your full deck.
 - Spring (Tarot): Enhances two (2) cards into Blossom cards.
 - Storm (Spectral): Duplicates all held in hand Blossom cards.

## Content:

### New Jokers!
This mod adds over 45 new jokers to the base game, ranging from common jokers like the English Chap, to legendaries which balatrify the main 5 guys in colorful ties you all know and love. Each joker has a unique and fun feature which may or may not be balanced... I'm not good at balancing lmao

### New consumables!
10 new comsumables come to Balatro, 5 spectrals and 5 tarots! Each tarot has its own unique feature, ranging from basic enhancers to even merging two cards together. Each spectral comes with its own powerful effect which could turn the tide of your game.

### Ties
In the Tie Update™, 6 new seals (referred to as "Ties") were added to the game, each designed to provide a minor synergy with their respective legendary joker!  
 - Red Tie: Joe Hawley
 - Blue Tie: Zubin Segishi
 - Green Tie: Andrew Horowitz
 - Yellow Tie: Rob Cantor
 - Gray Tie: Ross Federman
 - Orange Tie: Bora Karaca

### Enhancements
Tally Ballatro adds two new enhancements to Balatro: the Mechanical Card and the Blossom card! You can find out what these enhancements do in game.

### Edition
The Zirconium edition, based off of the song "Ruler of Everything," Tally Hall's 14th song in Marvin's Marvelous Mechanical Museum, is a basic xChips edition, working similarly to the Polychrome edition.
